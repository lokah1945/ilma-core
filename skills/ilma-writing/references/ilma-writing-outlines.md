# ILMA Blog Writing — Outline Templates

## 5 Outline Types

### 1. HOW-TO (Tutorial/Guide)

```
# [Title: Cara X — Step by Step]

## HOOK (100 words)
[Story/question/stat yang bikin pembaca stop scroll]
Contoh: "3 jam stuck di error yang sama? Aku juga pernah. Padahal solusinya cuma 5 menit."

## PEMAHAMAN DASAR (1 section)
- Apa yang akan kamu pelajari
- Prerequisites (tools, knowledge)
- Kenapa ini penting

## LANGKAH 1: [Sub-judul]
[Penjelasan detail]
[Code example / screenshot]
[Pitfall yang sering terjadi]

## LANGKAH 2: [Sub-judul]
...

## LANGKAH 3: [Sub-judul]
...

## KESALAHAN UMUM (1 section)
- Mistake 1 + how to fix
- Mistake 2 + how to fix

## KESIMPULAN + CTA
[Ringkas 3 poin utama]
[Specific action: "Coba Langkah 1 sekarang di project kamu"]

## RELATED POSTS
[2-3 internal links]
```

### 2. LISTICLE (Top 5/10)

```

## INTRO (100 words)
[Context + kenapa list ini penting]
[Janji: "Di akhir post ini, kamu akan tahu X"]

## #1 [Item dengan impact terbesar]
[Alasan kenapa ini #1]
[Contoh/contoh]
[Cara implementasinya]

## #2 [Item]
...

## #3 [Item]

## BONUS: [Item ke-N yang unexpected]
[Surprise element yang bikin share]

```

## KRITERIA 1: Kecepatan

Tool B: [result]

[Batas waktu berapa]

## STRATEGI YANG DIPAKAI

## LANGKAH 1: [Key insight #1]

### 5. OPINION/PENDING viewpoint

```
# [Title: [Strong Opinion] — dan ini kenapa saya percaya itu]

## HOOK
[Bold claim / controversial statement]
Contoh: "99% advice tentang X itu salah. Ini alasannya."

## KONTEKS
[Pengalaman/background yang mendasari opinion]
[Kenapa kamu qualified untuk speak tentang ini]

## ARGUMEN 1: [First pillar]
[Fakta/data/observation pendukung]
[Example]

## ARGUMEN 2: [Second pillar]
...

## ARGUMEN 3: [Third pillar]
...

## COUNTER-ARGUMEN + DEBUNK
["Tapi ada yang bilang X..." — dismantle with evidence]

## KESIMPULAN + CALL TO ACTION
[Strong, actionable ending]
[Invite for discussion]
```

## Outline Generation Rules

1. **Hook MUST be in first 100 words** — readers decide to continue in 3 seconds
2. **Every section needs a purpose** — if it doesn't advance the argument, remove it
3. **Subheaders every 300-400 words** — improves readability, SEO, social shareability
4. **CTA at end must be specific** — not "learn more" but "download template X" or "try this in your next project"
5. **Internal links embedded in context** — not just at the bottom
6. **Use Indonesian power words** — cara, rahasia, tips, trik, terbaru, teruji, lengkap
